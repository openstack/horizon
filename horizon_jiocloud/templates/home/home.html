Enabling Indian Innovators

JioCloud has been founded with a mission to enable you to utilize the best computing tools and resources. JioCloud enables quick access to flexible and low cost IT resources, on-demand via your Internet browser, be it for a simple mobile application, popular website or for business critical operations. JioCloud is built on open standards, thereby facilitates avoidance of being locked into a single Cloud Service Provider.

Blazing Performance

JioCloud has all its data centers in India, bringing down latency by manifold. Your application need not face delays in response due to host servers being located in a different continent. With JioCloud, you can experience much better performance.

No vendor lock-in

JioCloud supports open standards allaying your fears of being locked into a single vendor. With AWS API compatibility being offered, you can move your workloads from AWS to JioCloud seamlessly.

Pay per use

JioCloud offers highly transparent, flexible and simple pricing, allowing you to plan your budget and resources as per your usage. For instance, you can utilize resources on an hourly-price basis.

24x7 support

JioCloud believes that innovators must not have to worry about anything other than shipping their product ideas. Users can expect excellent support to be one-click away, at any time of the day either through live chat, phone or via e-mail.
